My name is Sybren de Graaff.

I’m an aspiring content and web-designer, who is in his second year of his studies. I study Communication and Multimedia Design at the University of Applied Sciences in Amsterdam.

Since I was young, I’ve been busy creating. From drawing portraits to making up stories with friends, I was always using my fantasy. So when the time came to choose what course to take, I knew I wanted to do something that would stimulate my right brain cells.

In my portfolio I will show the work I’ve done for school so far, and the drawings I’ve made over the years. Also the links to my social media will be included in this webpage.

Enjoy!
